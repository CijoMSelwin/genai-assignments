TC_BT_001 Title: Verify successful fund transfer within daily limit Preconditions: User is logged in with an active account and has sufficient balance Steps:
1. Navigate to Fund Transfer
2. Enter beneficiary details
3. Enter amount within daily limit
4. Submit transaction Expected Result: Transaction completes successfully and confirmation is shown
